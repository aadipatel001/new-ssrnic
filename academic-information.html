From the administrative point of view the school is divided broadly into two sections â€“ Lower Secondary Level (LSL) and Higher Secondary Level (HSL).

LSL
In classes 6 to 8, students undergo a structured academic program that covers a wide range of subjects to foster a well-rounded education.Our dedicated faculty members employ innovative teaching methodologies to make learning engaging and interactive. Regular assessments, class participation, projects, and assignments form an integral part of the evaluation process, ensuring continuous monitoring of students' progress.
HSL
As students progress to classes 9 to 12, they have the opportunity to choose from three distinct streams, aligning with their academic interests and career aspirations:

Science Stream: Core subjects include Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology (as per student preference). Optional subjects may include English, Hindi, Computer Science/Informatics Practices, and Physical Education.
ART Stream: Core subjects encompass History, Geography, and Political Science. Optional subjects may include English, Hindi, Economics, Sociology, Psychology, Home Science, Fine Arts, and Physical Education.
Humanities Stream: Core subjects consist of History, Geography, and Economics. Optional subjects may include English, Hindi, Political Science, and Physical Education. Our seasoned educators guide students through a rigorous academic curriculum, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and analytical skills. Assessment methods include theory examinations, practical assessments (where applicable), internal evaluations, projects, and presentations, adhering to the grading system prescribed by the Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education.
